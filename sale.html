Commission Structure & Marketing Fees

We work strictly on performance. That means we invest our administrative time, media materials, and outreach costs upfront. We are only compensated once a transaction closes.

5% Commission

For house sales, commercial towers, and agricultural plots. This standard rate is only charged once deeds transfer completely. It covers all physical buyer showings, digital brochure design, and registry searches. Special rate reductions are negotiable for exclusive multi-property portfolios.
