Financial & Mortgage Guidance

Need home finance? Tuli Ma partners with premier Zambian retail finance houses (including Stanbic Bank, FNB, ABSA, and Standard Chartered) to guide buyers through standard loan facilities.

Zambian Bank Financing

Mortgages in Zambia are typically offered in ZMW (Kwacha) or USD (restricted to foreign earners). Typical terms run 5 to 15 years.

  • • Rates: Variable linked to MPC rate
  • • Typical Deposit reqs: 10% to 20%

Pre-Approval Checklist

Always seek a pre-approval slip before placing outright purchases. This establishes credible standing for price negotiations.

  • • Payslips (Last 3 to 6 months)
  • • Audited Business Records (if self-employed)

Valuation Mandates

Zambian banks mandate detailed structural valuation reports from registered evaluers before releasing home capitals. Tuli Ma drafts these.

  • • Formal state certified valuations
  • • Clean Council planning clearances
