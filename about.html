The Story Behind Tuli Ma Property Brokers

Founded on the bedrock of complete administrative transparency, Tuli Ma Property Brokers emerged in response to the growing challenges surrounding land title validation and pricing inflation inside Zambia's metropolitan and commercial centers.

We observed that foreign investors, corporate firms, and local families struggled with unreliable broker networks. Tuli Ma set out to create a premium brokerage with a focused code of ethics: strict verification of title histories at the Ministry of Lands, absolute pricing clarity, and robust, data-backed advice.

Today, we operate as Lusaka’s preferred boutique real estate house, servicing neighborhoods including Kabulonga, Woodlands, Ibex Hill, Roma, Rhodes Park, and expanding land blocks in Leopards Hill.

"Our agency ensures you are never forced to compromise. We strive to deliver the right physical spaces, at the right commercial rate, at any given time."
